1. Get the Service URL on Minikube

  - Quick one-shot:
      minikube service {{ include "busybox-web.fullname" . }} --url

  - Or run a tunnel for LoadBalancer semantics:
      minikube tunnel

  Then check:
      kubectl get svc {{ include "busybox-web.fullname" . }}

2. Port info

  - Service port: {{ .Values.service.port }}
  - Container (http) port: {{ .Values.http.containerPort }}
